Who We Are

Our Medical Practice

We are licensed Psychiatrists with combined 30 years of medical experience, providing online consultation. Our specialization includes anxiety disorder, depression, insomnia, mood disorder, and post traumatic stress disorder in adults and children.

Our Treatment Focus

Our focus is to help individuals heal, energize, and become aware of their inner strengths. We achieve this by providing a neutral safe space, listening to your concerns, and customizing a treatment plan.

Our Patient Commitment

We promise to be there for you every step of your journey. Our goal is alleviate your unwanted symptoms, to help you grow from your struggles, heal from your pain, and move forward to where you want to be in your life.

Depression, Mood, Anxiety, and Panic Attacks

Depression, mood instability, fear, anxiety, insomnia, and panic are some of the most common and uncomfortable emotions that we can experience at some point in our lives. Through psychotherapy, counseling, and treatment, we are able to help you recover motivation, perspective, and joy that you once had in your life.

Trauma & PTSD

Many individuals can experience symptoms associated with painful and traumatic circumstances. Anxiety, fear, and hopelessness are a few emotions that can linger post traumatic events. We can help you overcome these symptoms and guide you through the process of grief and healing.

Note: This is NOT for any emergencies. For emergency cases, please seek consult at the nearest Hospital Emergency Room.
